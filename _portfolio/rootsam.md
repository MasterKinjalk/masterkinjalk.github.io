RootSAM adapts the Segment Anything Model (SAM2.1) to minirhizotron root imagery via two-stage fine-tuning and EDT-based prompt simulation. It achieves mIoU 66% (37% relative gain over the baseline) and cuts annotation cost by 91%, enabling high-quality root masks with minimal manual effort.

**Publication:** [RootSAM: Adapting SAM2.1 for Root Segmentation in Minirhizotron Imagery](/publication/2025-cvppa-rootsam) — CVPPA 2025 (ICCV Workshop).
