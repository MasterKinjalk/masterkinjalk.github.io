**RootSAM: Adapting SAM2.1 for Root Segmentation in Minirhizotron Imagery**

With Sebastian Quintela, Andrew Leakey, Sam Walker (University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign) and Nadia Shakoor (Donald Danforth Plant Science Center).

Presented as an extended abstract at the [Computer Vision for Plant Phenotyping and Agriculture (CVPPA) workshop](https://cvppa2025.github.io/proceedings/) at ICCV 2025 (October 20, 2025). RootSAM adapts the Segment Anything Model (SAM2.1) for root segmentation in minirhizotron imagery.
